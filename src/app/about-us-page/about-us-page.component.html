Our Story

We’re more than just a business; we’re a family tradition, with a craft honed and perfected over two generations. Our roots trace back to the early 1800s, when founder Mzamo Dikweni laid the foundation, passing the legacy to his son, Morris Dikweni. Due to unforeseen circumstances, the business closed in 1997, but thanks to overwhelming demand, it has been revived.

In 2022, over 20 years later, we resumed our journey to continue a legacy that could never be forgotten.

Congratulations, you're now fully up to speed! Join us on this story filled with highs and lows, and an even greater passion for all things meat.
