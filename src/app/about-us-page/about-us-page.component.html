Our Story

We are not just a business, we’re a family business, with a craft that has been improved and perfected over the course of 2 generations, our origins date back to the early 1800's. Founder Mzamo Dikweni, who passed a legacy to his son Morris Dikweni, due to unforeseen circumstances business had to closed in 1997

Over 20 years later in 2022 we picked up where we left off to carry on a legacy, that couldn’t be abandoned.

Congratulations you’re officially all caught up, join us along this story rich with ups and downs, and an even richer obsession with all things meat.
